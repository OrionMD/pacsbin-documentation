# Hello, v2!

##### April 28, 2026 - Jason Hostetter

## Migration is complete!
As of today, the next version of Pacsbin is available at
[pacsbin.com](https://pacsbin.com).

Pacsbin v1 has been switched to read-only mode and will remain available for
approximately 1 month.

I sincerely hope everyone enjoys the new features, better (in my opinion)
layouts, and (hopefully) increased speeds overall. That said, as with any new
rollout, especially one of this scale, I am sure that
there will be issues. When you encounter them, please report along with a
detailed description (including screenshots, video, whatever) to me at
support@pacsbin.com. You can also use the feedback form here: [https://pacsbin.com/feedback](https://pacsbin.com/feedback)

To help ease the transition, if you do find a showstopping bug or omission and
need to use the old version of Pacsbin until the problem is resolved, v1 of
Pacsbin will remain available at [v1.pacsbin.com](https://v1.pacsbin.com) for at
least one month **in read-only mode**. This means you will be able to log in and
view studies, but not edit them or upload new studies. If you find yourself in
this situation, please report your issue so that it can be resolved asap.

Thanks again to everyone for your support! Looking forward to building on this foundation.
